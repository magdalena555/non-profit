    Where we work

    After creating cohort chapters in Philadelphia; Cambridge, MA; Hartford, CT and Harlem, Say Yes began to offer its services to every public high school student across a community. Say Yes now has community-wide chapters in the New York cities of Syracuse and Buffalo, as well as Guilford County, North Carolina.

    learn more

    Higher Ed Partners

    Say Yes students have the opportunity to receive scholarships to colleges and other post-secondary institutions, whether two-year, four-year or vocational – public or private.

    learn more

    Partner With Us

    Efforts like Say Yes to Education need support from civic leaders, funders, institutions of higher education and policymakers to serve more cities and counties – all in pursuit of developing the gifts and talents of young people. We believe that when a community comes together, it has the capacity to effectively and sustainably redefine expectations and to build pathways to postsecondary education and careers.
